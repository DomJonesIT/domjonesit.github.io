NEWS, SCORES, & RUMORS

Scores

Thunder vs. Pelicans

Kevin Durant and Anthony Davis both miss key western conference matchup, but shorthanded Pelicans can't contain MVP candidate Russell Westbrook who scored a game high 43 points.

Cavaliers vs. Bucks

One day after questioning the urgency of his teammates, LeBron and Cavs defeat eastern conference playoff contender Bucks. James and Love combine for 42 points and Delly provides 13 points starting for Mo Williams.

Warriors vs. Clippers

Quickly becoming the best rivalry in the league, the Clippers couldn't give the Warriors their first loss of the season. Unlike their last few matchups, there were no fights or shoving matches.

Christmas Day Games

Cavs vs. Warriors

After suffering his fourth NBA Finals loss at the hands of Steph Curry and the Golden State Warriors, LeBron James and company hope to prove that they are a worthy opponent to the reigning champs. Will LeBron avenge his Finals loss and redeem his championship hopes? Find out Christmas Day at 5:00 pm.

Rockets vs. Spurs

James Harden and Dwight Howard have underachieved during their time as teammates. Will "The Beard" show the former champs what he's all about and will Dwight Howard ever be Superman again? We'll see during their 8:00 pm matchup Christmas Day.

Clippers vs. Lakers

The older Kobe Bryant gets, the more he looks like Old Man Winter. Will the remainder of this season be a gift or another lump of coal for Lakers fans? Tune in Christmas night at 10:30 pm to find out.
